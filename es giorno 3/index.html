Foccaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste budes with this festive appetizer topped with sweet caramelized oniions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sully Vegas

featured in:

fresh cranberry that go beyond sauce

ingredients

for the foccaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

for the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 table balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounce brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not neccesary)
  • 1 1/2 cups frsh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the foccaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
